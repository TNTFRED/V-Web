Actress Verity Marks was born in Wolverhampton, England. Moving to Canada in 2008 she began acting at age 5, undertaking many singing, dancing and musical theatre productions. Appearing in many feature films most recently being "Elevator Game"(2023), "Toys of Terror" (2020), and Lifetimes "Missing and Alone" (2021) Verity has also undergone training at London's leading drama school, Identity School Of Acting and continues to develop her skills both on and off screen.
